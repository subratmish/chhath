Nahay Khay - Day 1

Juhu Beach, Mumbai

The first day of Chhath Puja begins with ritual bathing and preparation. Devotees take holy dips and prepare for the four-day festival dedicated to Surya Dev and Chhathi Maiya.

OCT
Kharna - Day 2

Juhu Beach, Mumbai

Day of fasting and evening worship. Devotees observe nirjala vrat (waterless fast) and break it in the evening after offering kheer and chapati to Chhathi Maiya.

Sandhya Arghya - Day 3

Juhu Beach, Mumbai

Evening worship to the setting sun. The most significant day where devotees stand in water and offer arghya (water) to Surya Dev with traditional songs and prayers.

Main Ritual Learn More

Usha Arghya - Day 4

Juhu Beach, Mumbai

Final day with morning worship to rising sun. Devotees break their 36-hour fast after offering prayers to the rising sun, concluding the sacred Chhath Puja festival.

Event Guidelines

Important information for all festival participants

Timing

Festival events begin at 4:30 AM for morning rituals and 5:30 PM for evening ceremonies. Please arrive 30 minutes early.

Safety

Follow all safety guidelines while in water. Volunteers and lifeguards will be present. Children must be supervised at all times.

Environment

Use only eco-friendly materials. Avoid plastic items. Participate in our beach cleanup initiatives to keep Juhu Beach pristine.
