Tax Benefits

80G Tax Exemption

Donations are eligible for tax deduction under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. Tax-saving receipts will be issued for all donations.

  • 100% tax deductible
  • Official receipts provided
  • PAN card required
  • Instant email receipt

Secure & Safe

Your donation is processed through secure, encrypted channels. We never store your payment information.

Important: We will never ask for your payment details via phone or email. Always ensure you're on our official website before making any donation.
