About Chhath Utsav Mahasangh

Founded in 1993, Chhath Utsav Mahasangh is a cultural and social organization dedicated to preserving the sacred traditions of Chhath Puja. We strive to provide a safe, inclusive, and environmentally conscious environment for thousands of devotees each year.

Our journey began with a small group of volunteers and has grown into a large community-driven effort, ensuring the sanctity and grandeur of the festival for generations to come.

Message from the Founder

"Chhath Puja teaches us to be grateful for abundance, to uphold the sanctity of nature, and to help devotees, and strengthen bonds of community and come partway to who they provide every year."
