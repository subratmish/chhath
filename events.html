Festival Guidelines

For a safe and meaningful celebration

Safety Guidelines

  • Follow designated entry and exit points
  • Stay within marked safe zones in water
  • Keep emergency contact numbers handy
  • Report any emergency to volunteers
  • Carry minimal valuables

Environmental Guidelines

  • Use eco-friendly, biodegradable materials
  • Avoid plastic decorations and containers
  • Dispose of waste in designated bins
  • Participate in cleanup activities
  • Respect marine life and water bodies
