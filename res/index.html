Four Sacred Days

Each day holds special significance in the Chhath Puja tradition

1

Nahay Khay

October 25, 2025

Purification day with holy baths and sattvic food preparation.

2

Lohanda

October 26, 2025

Day-long fasting with evening prayers and kheer distribution.

3

Sandhya Arghya

October 27, 2025

Evening offerings to the setting sun - the most spectacular day.

4

Usha Arghya

October 28, 2025

Dawn prayers to welcome new beginnings and complete the vrat.
