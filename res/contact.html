Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do in case of emergency during the festival?

Use the emergency helpline +91 98765 43210 for medical emergencies, lost persons, security concerns, or any urgent assistance needed during the festival period. Our emergency response team is available 24/7 during the festival.

How can I volunteer for Chhath Puja?

Fill out our contact form with subject "Volunteer Opportunities" or email volunteer@chhathutsav.org. We'll get back to you with available roles and requirements. Volunteers are needed for various activities including crowd management, logistics, and assistance.

Is parking available at the festival location?

Yes, designated parking areas are available, but spaces are limited during the festival. We recommend using public transportation for easier access and to avoid traffic congestion.

What should I do if I lose something during the festival?

Report lost items immediately to our volunteer coordinators at the information desk or call our helpline. We maintain a lost and found service throughout the festival period.

Are there facilities for elderly and disabled devotees?

Yes, we provide wheelchair accessibility, dedicated seating areas, and volunteer assistance for elderly and disabled devotees. Contact us in advance for special arrangements.

Can I bring my own food and offerings?

Yes, devotees can bring their own prasad and offerings. We encourage the use of eco-friendly materials and biodegradable items in keeping with our environmental commitment.
