About Chhath Utsav Mahasangh

Three decades of preserving sacred traditions and serving the devotee community

Our Mission

Founded in 1993, Chhath Utsav Mahasangh is a cultural and social organization dedicated to preserving the sacred traditions of Chhath Puja. We strive to provide a safe, inclusive, and environmentally conscious environment for thousands of devotees each year.

Our mission extends beyond organizing festivals - we work year-round to promote cultural awareness, environmental sustainability, and community welfare among the Bihari diaspora in Mumbai.

Our Vision

To create a harmonious celebration that honors ancient traditions while embracing modern values

Cultural Preservation

Maintaining the authenticity and sanctity of traditional Chhath Puja rituals for future generations.

Community Unity

Bringing together people from all walks of life to celebrate our shared cultural heritage.

Environmental Care

Promoting eco-friendly practices and environmental consciousness in all our celebrations.

Our Journey

Three decades of service and dedication

1993

Foundation

Chhath Utsav Mahasangh was founded by a small group of dedicated volunteers from the Bihari community in Mumbai. The first celebration was held at Juhu Beach with just 200 devotees.

2000

Growth & Recognition

Crossed the milestone of 1,000 devotees participating in the four-day celebration. Established formal partnerships with local authorities and received official recognition.

2010

Infrastructure Development

Built permanent ghat facilities at Juhu Beach. Introduced safety protocols, medical aid stations, and organized volunteer coordination systems.

2020

Digital Transformation

Successfully conducted COVID-compliant celebrations with safety protocols. Launched digital platforms and live streaming for devotees unable to attend physically.

2024

Present Day

Now serving over 50,000 devotees annually with 500+ dedicated volunteers. Recognized as Mumbai's largest and most organized Chhath Puja celebration.
